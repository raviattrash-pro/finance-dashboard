# ✦ Aura Wealth | Predictive Behavioral Wealth Simulator

![Aura Wealth Hero Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x400/030712/fbbf24?text=Aura+Wealth+Terminal)

> A premium, client-side financial planning terminal built with Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Aura Wealth transcends basic budgeting by integrating institutional asset allocation, the 50/30/20 rule, F.I.R.E. (Financial Independence, Retire Early) trajectory calculations, and behavioral finance stress-testing into a single, seamless, glassmorphism UI.

---

## ✨ Core Features

Aura Wealth is divided into two primary financial engines, supported by an advanced behavioral suite.

### 1. Dual-Mode Financial Engine
* **Salary Splitter (50/30/20 Rule):** Dynamically allocates monthly income into Needs, Wants, and an automated Investment Engine. Automatically adjusts for dependent insurance scaling.
* **Lumpsum Engine (Age-Adjusted):** Deploys large capital injections using the "Rule of 120" to balance Equity (Large/Mid/Small/Flexi Cap), Fixed Income, and Gold hedging. 

### 2. Behavioral Psychology & Alerts
* **🚨 Debt Avalanche Protocol:** If user EMIs exceed 30% of their income, the app automatically suspends lifestyle spending and shifts capital into a highly aggressive debt payoff engine.
* **🚀 F.I.R.E. Calculator:** Instantly calculates the exact number of years until the user achieves total financial independence based on their current automated savings rate.
* **🧨 Market Crash Stress Test:** Simulates a brutal 30% equity market wipeout to visually prove the mathematical necessity of holding Fixed Deposits and Gold as anti-fragile shock absorbers.

### 3. Advanced Wealth Suite
* **☕ The Latte Factor (Micro-Habit Multiplier):** Calculates the 10, 20, and 30-year opportunity cost of cutting small, daily luxury expenses.
* **🏖️ Retirement Paycheck (SWP Simulator):** Applies the institutional "4% Trinity Rule" to calculate a safe, infinite monthly withdrawal rate from a target retirement corpus.
* **🏠 Rent vs. Buy Showdown:** Calculates the 15-year opportunity cost of a house downpayment vs. renting and investing the difference.

### 4. Interactive Data Visualization
* **10-Year Wealth Predictor:** Uses `Chart.js` to render an interactive compound interest curve.
* **10% Annual Step-Up:** Toggle to visualize the explosive power of wage-growth compounding.
* **Inflation Adjustment:** Toggle to strip away a 6% inflation rate to see the *real* purchasing power of future wealth.
* **Life Event Shocks:** Add a massive expense (e.g., a wedding or house deposit) at Year 5 to see how it alters the compounding trajectory.

### 5. Premium UI/UX & Global Customization
* **5 Dynamic Themes:** Midnight Obsidian, Ocean Blue, Cyber Neon, Pearl Ivory, and Emerald Wealth.
* **Multi-Currency Support:** Seamlessly swap between ₹ (INR), $ (USD), € (EUR), and £ (GBP) — the app automatically updates all index fund and ETF suggestions to match the region.
* **Native PDF Blueprint Export:** Generate and download a perfectly formatted A4 blueprint of your financial plan with a single click.
